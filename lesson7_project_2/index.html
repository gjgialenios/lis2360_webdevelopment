Why Social Media?

In todays age every large industry leading company is almost required to have a presence on social media. Company exposure through platforms like Instagram, Twitter and Facebook have become the most efficient way to gain following. A new age of marketing brought in by millennial’s is now widely accepted as the norm in 21rst century marketing strategy and implementation.

Social media connects you with 3 billion users from around the world. As either a small or large company this is fundamental to gaining awareness for your brand. Luckily for newer companies creating a page on any social media platform is user friendly and can be easily manipulated for promotional use.

One of the major benefits of social media marketing is create a distinct brand for your company. Branding refers to anything that uniquely identifies an organization. This can span from a logo, catch phrase, color scheme, image filter, etc. As a social media marketer it is your job to make sure the aesthetic and flow of your pages are distinct and "on brand" with your organization and are cohesive with its message.

Social media has grown from a simple past time to one of the most influential resources in the working industry around the world. Every major company understands the importance of having a social media presence, and even goes to the extent to hire full teams to manage and run their organizations accounts. Social media is the future of company marketing.
